1. Personal webpage to host a portfolio of your work in this class 

I believe the excellent work you do in this class (and other classes) should be thoroughly documented and shared online.   I had a discussion with a Google resume screener once, and she told me that one of the best things you can do in your resume is have links to real projects with documentation and code. 
To help you with this, your first assignment is to create a public webpage where you will share the work you do in this class.  If you already have a webpage, add a subpage or a section for this course.  At a minimum this page should have your name* and the name of the course.   I also recommend having a photo of yourself and a brief biography and a link to your resume, but this is optional.   * If you are uncomfortable sharing your name or other personal info online, you can use your first name and last initial.  Let me know if you have any concerns.  
You can use any hosting service or webpage creation service that you would like- Google sites, squarespace, wix...  I have used Google sites for this purpose in the past, and it works well for quickly sharing your work.  And it is free.  Note:  The goal here isn't to create a webpage from scratch.  The goal is to have a platform for quickly documenting your course work, through text, images, links and videos.
