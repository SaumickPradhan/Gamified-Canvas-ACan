Design Principles In-Class Activity-1

Canvas is a big improvement over the software that was prevalent when I was a student (Blackboard). 

But, maybe we can make it even simpler? 

1. In a small group, consider your Canvas page for a course- like my UI course.  Look at the buttons on the side.  What buttons do you actually use?  What don't you use?  

Go to this powerpoint presentation, link here: https://docs.google.com/presentation/d/1WE8JLCnx4R03llHnT6fsPO0P7RNIJmNoAAcXv3Q8_wo/edit?usp=sharing

Select a slide to edit, add your names in the comments. 

Can you shade out the features you rarely use?  Highlight the features you use frequently. 

2. What would you say is the core feature of canvas for your group. What do you actually use it for?  Can you write it down in a sentence or two?  Add it to the comments of the powerpoint. 

3. Try sketching an alternate canvas page, that focuses on these features.  Take a screen shot and add it to a new slide next to your original critique.
